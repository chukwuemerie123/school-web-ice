The Nigeria College of Aviation Technology (NCAT) is an NCAA Approved Training Organization
(ATO) Localized in Zaria Kaduna State.

Nigerian Civil Aviation center

We Are The Biggest College Of Aviation In Nigeria.

To provide training that will produce highly skilled and Advanced personnel that will serve as providers in the exchange of communication operations necessary for ensuring safety of air Navigation in the Aviation industry in accordance with ICAO standards.
Welcome to the Online Application Portal for Nigerian College of Aviation Technology. Through this portal, you can make payment and fill the Application Form. You can edit your Application Details, view your Application Profile and check your Application Status.

To register:
1. Read the Admission Requirements
2. Click on 'Register a new Account' at the right hand side to fill the registration form. NOTE: Registration is to be done once. If you have registered before just Login to continue filling your form.
3. Click on 'Login' to log on and generate a Transaction ID to make payments through.
4. On successful payment, log on to your profile and start filling the application form.
5. Edit your Application Profile (This will require your recent passport photograph and your biodata).
6. Edit your Institution Attended.
7. Edit your Qualifications (WASSCE, and other Certificates are entered here).
8. Select your choice of Course.
